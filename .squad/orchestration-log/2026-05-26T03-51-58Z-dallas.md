## Architecture Notes

- **Button navigation** (drill-in/drill-out): Uses short-lived Zustand handoff to preserve active managed filter across view boundary
- **Nav menu navigation**: Falls back to selector mount + app settings initialization (no handoff created)
- **Ref guards** (`customApiFilterWasChangedRef`, `catalogFilterWasChangedRef`): Prevent settings hydration from overwriting transferred state once consumed
- **Transient lifecycle**: `pendingManagedFilterHandoff` is cleared after destination selector consumes it, avoiding state leakage into subsequent nav-menu visits
